Explanation


This graph shows the common age of death amoung occupants in section I of the Brush Creek Cemetery. Each bar shows the total number of people who's age of death was in that age range. The blue part of the graph represents the amount of men and the pink part represents the number or women. The height of the graph overall displays the total amount of people who died in that age range. When you mouse over the bar, the table above shows the exact numbers.
